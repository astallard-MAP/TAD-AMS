# Cash 4 Houses: Content Safety & Zero-Tolerance Policy

## 1. Absolute Directives
The Cash 4 Houses portal is a safe, professional, and supportive environment. All content, whether generated by AI or submitted by users, must strictly adhere to these rules:

*   **No Hate Speech**: Zero tolerance for racism, sexism, or any form of discrimination based on protected characteristics.
*   **No Abuse**: Foul language, personal attacks, or aggressive behavior is prohibited.
*   **No Religious Content**: To maintain a neutral, inclusive environment, religious content (proselytizing, discussion, or symbols) is prohibited in all public and administrative communications.
*   **No Inappropriate/Mature Content**: Content must be "user-friendly" and suitable for all ages. No exceptions.
*   **Professionalism**: All communications must maintain a "warm, professional, and supportive" tone.

## 2. Enforcement (The AI Sentinel)
The 'Sentinel' AI Agent monitors all data entry points:
*   User Signups (Name, Email)
*   Property Valuation Requests
*   Chatbot Interactions (Andy Chat)
*   AI-Generated News & Social Media

## 3. Reporting & Audit
Any breach of these policies will trigger:
1.  Immediate entry into the **Security Audit Log**.
2.  Immediate notification to the **Global Administrator**.
3.  Automatic blocking or flagging of the offending content.
